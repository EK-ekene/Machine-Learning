"Empowering Biomedical Engineers Through Programming"

About

CodeRun is a coding bootcamp managed by the Ghana Society of Biomedical Engineers (GSBE), tailored specifically for biomedical engineering students. Our mission is to equip participants with essential programming skills, starting from Python basics to advanced data analysis and machine learning. Through collaborative learning and mentorship, CodeRun fosters a supportive community, empowering engineers to innovate at the intersection of technology and healthcare

Medical Imaging

Medical imaging uses technology to create visual representative of the body. Programming helps us enhance, analyze, and interpret these images, supporting accurate diagnostics and improved patient care

Signal processing

Signal processing lets us analyze biological data like brainwaves and heart rhythms. With programming, we can filter noise and find patterns, making it essential for diagnostics and healthcare innovation
